FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about MindTrack!

How do I make an account?

Simply click on the 'Sign Up' button at the top right-hand corner of the page and fill out the registration form to create an account for yourself!

How does MindTrack work?

We very carefuly assess and evaluate each users individual mental health through daily quizzes, assessments and projects and provide a user based platform where users can seek help and improve their mental health over time.

What do I do if I forgot my password?

If you've forgetten your password, just visit our Contact Us page and provide us with your account details and we will get back to you with help.
